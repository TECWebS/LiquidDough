150

⚖️ "Flour is a shapeshifter!" When using a 1 cup scoop, your flour can weigh anywhere from 110 to 150 grams. But with a kitchen scale, a gram is always a gram. Precision = perfect cookies!

120

🥖 The oldest bread starter is over 120 years old! Some bakers have passed down their sourdough starter like a family heirloom—it just needs regular feeding to live forever!

02

🍞 No 2 sourdough starters are the same. Each starter has its own unique blend of wild yeasts and bacteria, influenced by its environment—meaning even the same recipe can taste different in 2 places.

5,000
