Why We Love It

Why We Love Sourdough Starter (and Your Gut Will Too!)

Our bakes start with a live sourdough starter — aka nature's original probiotic magic. This bubbly blend of wild yeast and good bacteria helps break down gluten and phytic acid, making treats easier to digest and nutrients easier to absorb. It's naturally preservative-free, packed with flavor, and even supports a happy gut. Who knew something so simple could do so much?

The Science

Sourdough Starter

Ingredients

Whole Wheat Flour Water

Sourdough starter is a natural leavening agent made by fermenting flour and water. Unlike commercial yeast, it relies on wild yeasts and beneficial bacteria (mainly Lactobacillus) from the environment.

How It Works

Over time, these microorganisms develop into a bubbly, tangy mixture that not only helps dough rise but also enhances flavor and digestibility. Think of it as a living, breathing ingredient that brings both nutrition and personality to every bake!
